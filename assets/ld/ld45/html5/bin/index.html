Fall of Odin is the HTML5, atmospheric, rise to power game that you needed for this fall season! Play as a fallen Odin, battle other leaders to make their followers your own, and more!

Explore the dark underworld filled with mysterious totems of the gods, the ever watchful ravens, and potential friends and foes as you seek out to rebuild your following to re-ascend to godhood. BUT! How you achieve your goals will determine your final fame...

Controls

  • Move with WASD
  • Claim followers with E
  • Swing your mighty axe with SPACE
  • Guide your followers with the mouse
